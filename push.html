Push Day

Shoulders/Tris/Chest

Ex: 4x8-10 (4 sets of 8-10 reps)

*Reminder: Always do a warm-up set to avoid injury*

1. Bench Press

Lay down on the bench and position your hands about shoulder width apart. Push the barbell up and bring it down until your arms make a 45-70 degree angle. Repeat!

2. Skull Crushers

Lay down on a bench with either dumbbells or a barbell. Extend your arms out so that the weight is above your chest. Bring your arm to a 90 degree angle and slowly lower the weight about an inch above your forehead, hinging at the elbows. Bring the weight back up, still hinging at the elbows. Repeat!

3. Lateral Raises

Grab two dumbbells, standing or sitting, bring your arms out next to your body. Try not to hyperextend your arms.

4. Shoulder Press

Sit down on a bench and grab two dumbbells. Bring the weights up to your shoulder level and press up.

5. Tricep Pulldown

Using a cable machine, adjust the height so that the attachment is at your chest/slightly above. A common attachment is a rope. Hinging at the elbows, pull the cable down, engaging the triceps. Make sure not to move any other part of your arm! Slowly bring your arm back up and repeat.
