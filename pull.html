Pull Day

Back/Biceps

Ex: 4x8-10 (4 sets of 8-10 reps)

*Reminder: Always do a warm-up set to avoid injury*

1. Lateral Pull Down

2. Cable Machine Row

3. Bent-Over Row

4. Bicep Curl

Grab two dumbbells and bring your elbows close to your body. Make sure your arms are stationary and and curl the dumbbells one at a time, rotating your wrists outwards while hinging at the elbows.

5. Hammer Curl

Similar to the bicep curl, grab two dumbbells and bring your elbows close to your body. Instead of rotating your wrists outwards, hold the dumbbells directly outward, and curl one at a time.

Tip: hold the dumbbells kind of like you're holding a hammer.
